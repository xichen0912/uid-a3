Description
Lachenalia Quadricolour (Lachenalia aloides var quadricoloura), also known as ‘Soldier Boys’, is a stunning, low-maintenance plant that adds vibrant color and elegance to any space. With its unique gradient blooms in shades of red, orange, yellow with dark tips, this compact beauty is perfect for indoor or outdoor settings. Thriving in winter and spring, it’s a seasonal showstopper that brightens up your home or garden with minimal care. Rare and captivating, the Lachenalia quadricolor is the perfect way to elevate your plant collection or gift something truly special. Don’t miss out on this botanical gem!

Common Name: LACHENALIA

Also known as: Soldier Boys

Botanical Name: Lachenalia

Family Name: Hyacinthaceae

Plant Type: Bulb

Size: 20cm H x 10cm W

Planting Time: Autumn

Planting Width: 7cm Depth: 5cm

Flowering: Spring

Positioning: Full sun to light shade

General Information: Suitable for all climates except tropical. Will tolerate temperatures down to 0°C.

Watering: Water when the growth appears and keeps the soil slightly wet until the foliage dies off after flowering. When the bulbs are dormant keep them relatively dry.

Fertilising: Animal manure or compost should be worked through soil before planting.

Soil: Will grow in any well-drained soil.

Lifting & Storage: Bulbs can be left in the ground for many years.

Pests: Generally free from pests and diseases.

Tips: Excellent for borders, drifts or pots and containers. Combines beautifully with blue Muscari.

An extremely easy to grow houseplant, Monstera Fruit Salad Plant (Monstera deliciosa) gets it’s wonderful common name from the deliciously aromatic edible fruit that this plant will bear once it matures. This rainforest plant will happily grow indoors with a minimum of care and will climb along a stake, moss stick or trellis creating a beautiful display. As this plant matures, the large leathery leaves will grow into an extremely handsome feature and will also develop the trademark shape that gives this plant it’s other common name of Swiss Cheese Pant.
